## ADL Focuses on:
- Learning the simulation tool, analysing astrophysical systems and other astrophysics principles.
- Learning from fundamentals to moderate level learning
- Understanding the dynamics and integrator performance in the system
- Applications like tidal effects, resonance effects, planetary resonance, Saturn's rings, black hole formation and so on.

## Overview:
- Astro-Dynamics Lab uses computational methods to study astrophysical systems.
- It simulates planetary and moon motions and explores orbital dynamics.
- Different numerical integrators are tested for accurate simulations.
- The lab also studies phenomena like tidal effects, resonances, Saturn’s rings, and black hole formation.
